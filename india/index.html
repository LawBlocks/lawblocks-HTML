About

Law Blocks

Law Blocks is a one window blockchain-based ecosystem for creating, keeping records of contracts, and solving disputes providing unbiased, swift & affordable resolutions through arbitration.

  • Law blocks is a Legal Tech Community
  • It creates your smart legal contract using blockchain technology with digital signatures
  • LBT (Law Blocks Token) is the payment token of Law Blocks eco system
  • Law Blocks Token is an XRC-20 utility token, powered by XDC Blockchain Network
  • Virtual contracts with digital signatures, 100% valid and paperless

Features

Law Blocks Features

Law Blocks creates your smart legal contracts In just three steps, Law Blocks solves disputes through arbitration online.

Smart Legal Contracts

  • Step 1

    Create

    Create your Smart legal contracts from scratch and/or choose from a list of templates drafted by Law Blocks Legal Team, Select Law Blocks for dispute resolution.

  • Step 2

    Compose

    Insert clauses as for specific contracts, send notifications to the other party to agree on individual clauses and to mutually conclude on terms and conditions of the agreement.

  • Step 3

    Digital Signature

    Digitally pay by LBT sign cryptographically with the other part to activate your contract and save in Blockchain.

Dispute Resolutions

  • Step 1

    Disputes

    Connect and choose Law Blocks for your dispute resolution.

  • Step 2

    Choose Dispute Resolution

    Open and manage your dispute completely online with community & court layer.

  • Step 3

    Mediation

    Law Blocks resolves conflict through the use of communication and negotiation techniques.

  • Step 4

    Arbitration

    Agree with your Opposite part to trigger self-executing contract and resolve your disagreement with arbitration in an easy and affordable way.
